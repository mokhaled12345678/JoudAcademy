    Is online Quran teaching helpful? 
    Our experience finds 1 to 1 online Quran teaching effective, engaging and useful.Try your free trial session under no obligation to form your own opinion about its usefulness. 

    Are you providing these lessons from a CD or software? 
    We are providing online teaching service. Our website is used to provide information for our services. Actual teaching is conducted by a live teacher at agreed upon time. You can read learning material and Quran lessons from our website.

    Our representative will guide you and your child through the basic installation of the software. It takes less than a minute to be online and start taking classes. You need very basic familiarity with a computer and internet. You do not need any computer expertise to take lessons.
